Approach

My work is driven by a core belief in the transformative power of education and that technology is key to making education accessible.

For over a decade, I've delivered this value across a wide range of organisations - from global EdTech companies and international publishing houses to public institutions and NGOs. This multi-faceted experience has given me a unique, 360-degree perspective on the challenges and opportunities in the learning sector. It has equipped me to be a versatile leader who can navigate different cultures and bridge the gap between content, technology, business objectives and institutional constraints.

In 2025, I decided to found Mind / Map to apply that experience with precision and impact. I go where I am most needed and work with you in two ways: as a strategic advisor to draw the map and as an embedded fractional leader to guide the undertaking.
